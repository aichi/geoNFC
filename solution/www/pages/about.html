Privacy Policy

This application does not share any personal information or usage data.
The location service is for determining your position. Your location will not be tracked or saved.

Additional Notes

You will need pre-formatted NFC tags to use this app. Windows Phone is not able to read unformatted tags.

You can store map tiles for offline usage. Afterwards there will be 5 zoom layers available and you can use the app without a internet connection.
